PORTFOLIO

Thermal Power Plant

A thermal power plant maintenance engineer is responsible for ensuring the safe and efficient operation of a thermal power plant. They work to maintain and repair equipment such as turbines, boilers, generators, pumps, and other machinery that is used to generate electricity from thermal sources such as coal, natural gas, and oil.

Subject Matter Expert

A Subject Matter Expert (SME) in customer service is a highly skilled and experienced professional who provides expert guidance and support in the field of customer service. They are responsible for analyzing, evaluating, and improving customer service operations to ensure that they are aligned with the organization's goals and objectives.

Associate Customer Service

An Associate in customer service is an entry-level position responsible for providing support to customers with their inquiries, concerns, or issues. Associates in customer service act as the first point of contact for customers and are responsible for ensuring that they receive prompt and efficient service.
